We bring peace of mind while you're away from home.

What.

Can we do for you

Peace of Mind offers comprehensive home watch & inspection services for your home, while you are away. We offer customized services to fit your needs either seasonally or all year long. We provide a complete report after each visit that includes any issues discovered while inspecting the property. We take care of your home and property so that you can relax, knowing your home is in good hands while you're away.

Who.

Is in the team

Jeremy, Stephanie, Ansley and Taylor are proud to call Summerville, SC their home. Jeremy has been in the property management business for over 20 years. Stephanie has dedicated her professional life to taking care of others and providing excellent customer service.

Why.

Us and not them

With their combined experience, attention to detail and passion for helping people, the Williams family started Peace of Mind Home Watch Service. They are excited to get to know you and take care of your home as you would, while you are away!

Services.

Essential Package ($50/visit):

  • Verify doors & windows are locked
  • Check for evidence of water leaks or damage (visual signs of mold or mildew)
  • Visual inspection for signs of rodents or insects
  • Check & reset thermostats
  • Change air filters monthly (provided by client)
  • Run faucets, sinks, disposal & flush toilets
  • Check main water valve is turned off after each inspection
  • Check electric panel for tripped breakers & make sure water heater breaker is off
  • Visual inspection throughout property (including patio & garage)
  • Walk exterior perimeter to check screens, signs of vandalism, etc.

Plus Package ($60/visit):

  • All Essential services
  • Home access to contractors, vendors/service providers
  • Coordinate maintenance or repair services
  • Waiting for services (repairs, service, installations, delivery, etc.)
  • Package services
  • Rubbish services
  • Serving as client’s liaison with licensed general contractors & licensed specialty contractors
  • Mail services
  • Pre & post-storm inspection

Ultimate Package ($75/visit):

  • All Essential & Plus services
  • Personal vehicle & golf cart inspection
  • Inspect pool water level & clarity
  • Look at pool pump & heater for obvious signs of malfunction
  • Interior plant watering

Custom Package:

  • Additional custom requests available on request
